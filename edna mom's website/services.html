Live-In Care Plan

A Carer/ Carers live with you in your home, offering round-the-clock support for peace of mind.

Features

  • Carers are assigned a space/room within the Client's premises for ease of monitoring
  • Personal Care
  • Housework
  • Assistance with Medication
  • Running Errand (Not applicable if client lives alone)
  • Accompanying clients to appointments.
  • Mobility support
  • Outdoor outing with clients
  • Taking Vitals
  • Meal preparation

The above are subject to change based on the extent of care an individual client requires. If the Client is Dependent or a Semi-dependent then the above are properly adhered to. Also depending on the assessment of the client and the specific care plan considered to be adequate for him/her.
